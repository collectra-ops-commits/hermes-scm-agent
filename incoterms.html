Incoterms 2020

EXW - Ex Works

Definition: The seller makes the goods available at their own premises. The buyer bears all costs and risks involved in taking the goods from the seller's premises to the desired destination.

Risk Transfer: At seller's premises.

Seller ---*Buyer

Usage Guidance: Suitable for domestic trade. Buyer has maximum responsibility.

FCA - Free Carrier

Definition: The seller delivers the goods to the carrier or another person nominated by the buyer at the seller's premises or another named place.

Risk Transfer: At the named place of delivery to the carrier.

Seller --- Carrier ---*Buyer

Usage Guidance: Flexible for any mode of transport. Common for containerized cargo.

FAS - Free Alongside Ship

Definition: The seller delivers when the goods are placed alongside the vessel nominated by the buyer at the named port of shipment.

Risk Transfer: When goods are alongside the vessel.

Seller --- alongside Ship ---*Buyer

Usage Guidance: For sea or inland waterway transport only. Typically for heavy or bulky goods.

FOB - Free On Board

Definition: The seller delivers the goods on board the vessel nominated by the buyer at the named port of shipment or procures the goods already so delivered.

Risk Transfer: When goods are on board the vessel.

Seller --- on board Ship ---*Buyer

Usage Guidance: For sea or inland waterway transport only. Commonly used, but often misunderstood.

CPT - Carriage Paid To

Definition: The seller delivers the goods to the carrier nominated by the seller at an agreed place (if any) and the seller must contract for and pay the costs of carriage necessary to bring the goods to the named place of destination.

Risk Transfer: When goods are delivered to the first carrier.

Seller --- Carrier ---*Destination

Usage Guidance: For any mode of transport. Cost and risk transfer at different points.

CIP - Carriage and Insurance Paid To

Definition: Same as CPT, but the seller also pays for the insurance coverage against the buyer's risk of loss or damage to the goods during the carriage.

Risk Transfer: When goods are delivered to the first carrier.

Seller --- Carrier ---*Destination (Insured)

Usage Guidance: For any mode of transport. Seller arranges minimal insurance.

CFR - Cost and Freight

Definition: The seller delivers the goods on board the vessel nominated by the buyer at the named port of shipment or procures the goods already so delivered. The seller must contract for and pay the costs and freight necessary to bring the goods to the named port of destination.

Risk Transfer: When goods are on board the vessel at port of shipment.

Seller --- on board Ship ---*Destination Port

Usage Guidance: For sea or inland waterway transport only. Risk transfers at shipment, cost at destination.

CIF - Cost Insurance and Freight

Definition: Same as CFR, but the seller also pays for the insurance coverage against the buyer's risk of loss or damage to the goods during the carriage to the port of destination.

Risk Transfer: When goods are on board the vessel at port of shipment.

Seller --- on board Ship ---*Destination Port (Insured)

Usage Guidance: For sea or inland waterway transport only. Seller arranges minimal insurance.

DAP - Delivered at Place

Definition: The seller delivers the goods when they are placed at the disposal of the buyer on the arriving means of transport ready for unloading at the named place of destination.

Risk Transfer: At the named place of destination.

Seller -------------------*Destination

Usage Guidance: For any mode of transport. Seller bears most risks and costs.

DPU - Delivered at Place Unloaded

Definition: The seller delivers when the goods, once unloaded from the arriving means of transport, are placed at the disposal of the buyer at a named place of destination.

Risk Transfer: At the named place of destination, after unloading.

Seller ------------------- Unloaded *Buyer

Usage Guidance: For any mode of transport. Seller is responsible for unloading.

DDP - Delivered Duty Paid

Definition: The seller delivers the goods when the goods are placed at the disposal of the buyer, cleared for import on the arriving means of transport ready for unloading at the named place of destination.

Risk Transfer: At the named place of destination, cleared for import.

Seller ------------------- Cleared *Buyer

Usage Guidance: For any mode of transport. Seller has maximum responsibility, including duties and taxes.
